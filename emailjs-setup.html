1 Create EmailJS Account

EmailJS is a FREE service that sends emails from your browser without a backend server.


Go to EmailJS.com ‚Üí

  1. Click "Sign Up Free"
  2. Sign up with Google, GitHub, or Email
  3. Verify your email address
2 Connect Email Service

In your EmailJS dashboard:


  1. Click "Add New Service"
  2. Choose "Gmail" (recommended - easiest setup)
  3. Click "Connect Account"
  4. Sign in with your Gmail and allow access
  5. ‚úÖ Service created!

üìù Note: Copy your Service ID (looks like: service_abc123). You'll need this!
3 Create Email Template

Create a beautiful email template:


  1. Go to "Email Templates"
  2. Click "Create New Template"
  3. Copy the template from EMAILJS_SETUP.md
  4. Set Template ID to: template_verification
  5. Click "Save"

Template Variables to use:

{{to_name}}, {{to_email}}, {{verification_code}}, {{expiry_time}}, {{site_name}}
4 Get Your Public Key

Get your API credentials:


  1. Go to "Account" ‚Üí "General"
  2. Find your "Public Key"
  3. Copy the key (looks like: AbC123XyZ...)
5 Configure Your Application

Open email-verification.js and update lines 7-9:

this.serviceID = 'service_abc123'; // Your Service ID this.templateID = 'template_verification'; // Your Template ID this.publicKey = 'AbC123XyZ...'; // Your Public Key

6 Test It!
  1. Open signup.html
  2. Fill the form with your real email
  3. Click "Create Account"
  4. Check your email inbox for the 6-digit code
  5. Enter the code and verify!
